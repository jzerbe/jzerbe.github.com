The Lead

A generalist focused on getting to "Yes". My career has been defined by a transition from the abstract to the tangible - moving from designing data architectures to optimizing the physical flow of goods across borders. Whether it is a data pipeline or a supply chain, the core interest remains the same: Systems. I am analytical at the high level, intuitive at the low level, and unapologetically unconventional in my approach.

The Architecture of a Career

I spent 15 years building software that powers modern industry. {{ site.sidebar.linkedin.link }} My work has always been at the intersection of complex data and high-stakes decision-making:
  • Intelligence & Governance: At Immuta, I designed data provisioning solutions for enterprise clients, leading evaluations to ensure stakeholders made the right buying decisions for their data platforms.
  • AI & Search: As a Technical Product Owner at Koverse, I led the development of AI-guided search products and built operational processes for intelligence products designed for non-technical decision-makers.
  • Energy & Environment: At Tendril Networks, I managed the launch of novel machine learning technology for smart-thermostat load shifting. This system reduced business costs by 80% and secured multi-million dollar contracts with major utilities.
  • Compliance & Scale: At Workiva, I contributed to the Digital Review suite used for SEC and SOX compliance, helping scale a platform where usage tripled during my tenure.

The Current Venture: Vraid Systems

In 2020, I took my background in data engineering and applied it to the "dirt" and "grit" of global logistics. As the General Manager of Vraid Systems, I focus on delivering consumer electronics, telecom equipment, and lab disposables faster and more efficiently than traditional players.

My goal is to strip away the friction from physical trade using the same rigor I once applied to software re-architecture.
